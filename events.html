2022 Events

Unity Night

August 2022
Wellington College Memorial Hall

WISA co-hosted a multicultural show to celebrate cultural diversity, featuring performances like Kapa Haka and Chinese Lion Dancing.

Orientation Day

July 2022
Wellington Museum

After two years of border closure, WISA welcomed over 140 students from 8 countries and 14 schools with museum and sightseeing tours.

Bowling Night

June 2022
The Lanes

WISA organised its second Bowling Night so students from all over the city could enjoy a fun night out with friends and compete for prizes.

Blue Dragon Book Fair

May 2022
Ngaio Town Hall

The WISA committee volunteered at the Blue Dragon Book fair and helped with categorising donated books and recycling card boxes.

Picnic & Lasertag

April 2022
Waitangi Park

We met in Waitangi Park for a shared picnic, volleyball and frisbee games, followed by arcade games and lasertag at Laserforce Wellington.

Runathon Foodstall

April 2022
Wellington College

WISA run a food stall at Runathon, which raises funds various charities. We sold over 250 dumplings & samosas and raised $200 NZD.

2021 Events

Movie Fundraiser

November 2021
Penthouse Cinema, Brooklyn

We collaborated with Interact Club to raise funds for Blue Dragon Children's Foundation. 60 students joined us for the movie Shang Chi.

Movie Night

July 2021
Wellington College

Over 100 students joined us at Wellington College for the movie Weathering with You, Domino's Pizza, and cup-sized popcorn.

Roller Skating Night

June 2021
Kilbirnie Recreation Centre

Over 60 students attended our roller skating event filled with adrenalined-fuelled games like roller skate hockey, and relay race.

Bowling Night

April 2021
The Lanes

We organised a "bowling league" and randomised 40+ students from all over Wellington into teams and compete. We left no pin standing!

Sports Day

March 2021
Wellington College

After football matches, tug of war, and hot dogs for afternoon tea, the WISA 2021 election took place, where all candidates gave their speeches.
